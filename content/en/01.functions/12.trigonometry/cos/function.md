**Description**

Calculates the cosine of an angle (in radians). The result will be
between -1 and 1.

**Syntax**

`cos(rad)`

**Parameters**

`rad`: The angle in radians. Allowed data types: `float`.

**Returns**

The cos of the angle. Data type: `double`.
