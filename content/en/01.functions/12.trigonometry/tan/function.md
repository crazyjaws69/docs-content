**Description**

Calculates the tangent of an angle (in radians). The result will be
between negative infinity and infinity.

**Syntax**

`tan(rad)`

**Parameters**

`rad`: The angle in radians. Allowed data types: `float`.

**Returns**

The tangent of the angle. Data type: `double`.
